About Us

Pure. Safe. Refreshing.

SHIVPARIVAR FOODS & BEVERAGE is a food and beverage company committed to providing high-quality packaged drinking water that meets safety standards and customer expectations. We focus on purity, hygiene, and consistency in every product we deliver.

Our packaged drinking water is processed using advanced purification and ozonization techniques and enriched with permitted minerals. We follow hygienic bottling practices and strict quality control measures to ensure freshness and safety in every bottle.

Our Mission

To deliver clean, safe, and refreshing drinking water through reliable purification processes and responsible manufacturing practices.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and recognized name in the packaged drinking water industry by consistently maintaining quality and customer trust.

Quality Commitment

We prioritize quality at every stage — from purification to packaging — ensuring compliance with food safety standards and hygienic practices.
